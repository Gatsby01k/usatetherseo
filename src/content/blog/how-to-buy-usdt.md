## Step 1: Choose an Exchange

Look for:
- strong liquidity
- clear fee structure
- regulatory compliance in your region

See overview: [Best Exchanges for USDT](/blog/best-exchanges-for-usdt/)

---

## Step 2: Deposit Funds

Options:
- bank transfer
- debit/credit card
- crypto deposit

Card purchases usually have higher fees.

---

## Step 3: Choose the Trading Pair

Example:
- USDT/USD
- USDT/EUR
- USDT/BTC

---

## Step 4: Use Limit Orders (Recommended)

If liquidity is thin:
- avoid market orders
- reduce slippage

---

## Step 5: Choose Network for Withdrawal

Compare:
- ERC20
- TRC20

Details here: [USDT TRC20 vs ERC20](/blog/usdt-trc20-vs-erc20/)

---

## Common Mistakes

- withdrawing on wrong network
- ignoring fees
- using low-liquidity venues

## FAQ

### Can I buy USDT without KYC?
Depends on exchange policies and jurisdiction.

### What are typical fees?
Trading fees + withdrawal fees + network fees.
