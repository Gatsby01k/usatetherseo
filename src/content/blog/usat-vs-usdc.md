## Overview

Both USAT and USDC aim to track $1.  
Differences usually appear in liquidity and regulatory positioning.

---

## Comparison Table

| Feature | USAT | USDC |
|----------|------|------|
| Peg | USD | USD |
| Liquidity | Varies | High |
| Transparency | Depends on issuer | Regular attestations |
| Exchange Support | Limited/Developing | Very broad |

---

## When to Choose USAT

- Specific ecosystem usage
- Particular exchange listing

## When to Choose USDC

- Maximum liquidity
- Broad DeFi compatibility

---

## Risks

Both carry:
- counterparty risk
- regulatory risk
- market liquidity risk
