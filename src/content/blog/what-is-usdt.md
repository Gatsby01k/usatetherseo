## What USDT is
**USDT (Tether)** is a USD-pegged stablecoin widely used as a trading and transfer unit in crypto markets.

## Why it’s popular
- widely listed across exchanges
- deep liquidity
- used as a “base currency” for trading pairs

## Risk checklist
- issuer policies (freeze/blacklist possibilities)
- backing / transparency practices
- regulatory constraints in your region
- chain choice (fees differ a lot)

## Practical tips
- prefer low-fee networks for transfers
- avoid bridges unless you must
- keep track of withdrawal fees per exchange
