## TRC20

Pros:
- low fees
- fast confirmations

Cons:
- smaller DeFi ecosystem

---

## ERC20

Pros:
- strong security track record
- deep DeFi integration

Cons:
- higher gas fees during congestion

---

## Which Should You Use?

For:
- cheap transfers → TRC20
- DeFi usage → ERC20

Always confirm the receiving wallet supports the network.

---

## Safety Tip

Send a small test transaction before large transfers.
