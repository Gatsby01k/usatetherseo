## Main Risks

### 1. Depeg Risk
If panic selling occurs, price may fall below $1.

### 2. Reserve Risk
Backing assets may not be fully transparent.

### 3. Regulatory Risk
Government restrictions can limit operations.

### 4. Smart Contract Risk
Bugs or hacks can compromise funds.
