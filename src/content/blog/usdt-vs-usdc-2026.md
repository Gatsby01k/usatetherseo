## Overview

USDT and USDC are the two dominant USD stablecoins.

Both aim for $1, but differ in:
- transparency
- regulatory positioning
- ecosystem integration
- liquidity footprint

---

## Liquidity Comparison

| Feature | USDT | USDC |
|----------|------|------|
| Global liquidity | Extremely high | Very high |
| Exchange coverage | Very broad | Broad |
| Trading pair dominance | Strong | Moderate |

USDT remains dominant in many global trading markets.

---

## Transparency

USDC typically emphasizes:
- regular attestations
- clearer reserve disclosures

USDT publishes reserve information but has historically faced scrutiny.

---

## DeFi Usage

USDC often integrates deeply into:
- regulated DeFi platforms
- institutional products

USDT is more common in:
- global centralized exchanges
- high-liquidity pairs

---

## Risk Comparison

| Risk | USDT | USDC |
|------|------|------|
| Issuer control | Yes | Yes |
| Regulatory pressure | Moderate | Higher in US markets |
| Liquidity risk | Low | Low |

## Which Should You Choose?

Choose based on:
