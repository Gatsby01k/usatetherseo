## Before Buying

- Verify exchange
- Check liquidity
- Confirm contract

---

## Before Storing

- Backup seed phrase
- Use hardware wallet for large funds
- Enable 2FA

---

## Before Sending

- Confirm network
- Double-check address
- Send test transaction
