## Storage Options

### 1. Hardware Wallets
Best for long-term holding.
Examples:
- Ledger
- Trezor

Pros:
- Offline security

Cons:
- Less convenient

---

### 2. Software Wallets

- MetaMask (if supported network)
- Trust Wallet
- Bitget Wallet
- OKX Wallet
- Coin98 Wallet
- Uquid 

Pros:
- Easy access

Cons:
- Online exposure

---

### 3. Exchange Storage

Convenient but:
- you don’t control keys
- exchange risk exists

---

## Rule of Thumb

Large amounts → hardware wallet  
Trading funds → exchange  
Small operational funds → mobile wallet
