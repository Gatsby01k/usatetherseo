## Definition

A depeg happens when a stablecoin trades significantly above or below $1.

---

## Causes

- panic selling
- liquidity shortage
- reserve concerns
- regulatory shocks

---

## Example

If price drops to $0.95, it means confidence or liquidity is weak.
